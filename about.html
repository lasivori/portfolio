Louis Sivori

I am an undergaduate studying Computer Science. Why did I choose this? Honestly, it began with my love for video games. I was amazed at how these programs were presented to the players, and always wanted to see how things worked behind the curtain. So, I decided to start trying to learn how to code. I wasn't too good at it in my high school years, and of course I decided to put much more energy into socializing and playing video games. But, still wanting to learn how to program, I went to University of New Orleans. At first, I kept with a general computer science degree, since I didn't know if I truely wanted to go into game development. Eventually, I took a class that made me fall in love with cybersecurity, and therefor persued that path. Even though I did choose this specification, I still am learning how to make my own video games, and am also trying to branch out and learn even more (including web development).
